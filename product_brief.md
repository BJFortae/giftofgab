# GiftOfGab Product Brief

## Overview
GiftOfGab is a website concept designed to help job seekers uncover open roles through their existing network instead of relying only on cold applications.

## Problem
I’m never going to get a job by just applying to roles without knowing anyone at the company.

## Objective
Create a website that leverages my digital network to identify open roles, show who I know at the company, and limit those connections to 1st- or 2nd-degree relationships.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Must be easy to use and easy to onboard.
2. Should support direct SSO connection to LinkedIn to leverage the power of the digital network.
3. Can include personal contacts import to determine who I know personally (for example, people whose phone number I have) but am not connected with on LinkedIn.
